What is Fiber to the Home?

The gold standard in residential internet connectivity

FTTH (Fiber to the Home) delivers internet directly to your residence using fiber-optic cables—the same technology used by major data centers worldwide. Unlike traditional copper or wireless connections, fiber provides:

  • Symmetrical speeds – Upload as fast as you download
  • Low latency – Ideal for gaming and video calls
  • Future-proof infrastructure – Supports speeds beyond 1Gbps
  • Weather-resistant – No signal degradation in rain or heat
