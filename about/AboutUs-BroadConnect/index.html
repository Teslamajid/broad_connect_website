About Us

Our story, mission, and commitment to Sierra Leone

Our Story

BroadConnect was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to bridge the digital divide in Sierra Leone. What started as a small operation in Freetown has grown into the nation's most trusted internet service provider.

Since our founding, we've invested heavily in infrastructure, bringing fiber-optic technology to communities that once had limited connectivity options. Today, we serve over 50,000 customers across 15 districts.

Our journey is far from over. We continue to expand our network, innovate our services, and work towards a future where every Sierra Leonean has access to fast, reliable internet.
